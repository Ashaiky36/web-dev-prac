Paragraph Tag

The <p> tag defines a paragraph. Browsers automatically add a single blank line before and after each <p> element.

example

<!DOCTYPE html>
<html lang="en">
<head>
<title>Title of the document</title>
</head>
<body>
<p>London is the capital city of England.</p>
</body>
</html>
